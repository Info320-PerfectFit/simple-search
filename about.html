About Us

Here at Perfect Fit, we want you to get active.

We are a specialized search service for fitness novices. We are inspired by the people who get looked over when you think about fitness - people who are interested and intrigued by the idea of being physically fit, but simply don’t know how to go about doing so.

We offer a wide range of filtered sections, but with limited scope; breaking down each workout by type and then by body parts.

If you’re afraid of getting laughed at in the gym, you can filter by ‘at home’ workouts. If you only have 5 or 10 minutes to get active, check out our quick workouts section.

In order to attain relevant results, we crawled all the fitness websites we knew, and indexed them.
